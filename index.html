Our Promise

We built AvinyaHealth so doctors can care, not click. Our mission is to empower every clinic in India to go paperless, secure, and connected — without complexity. We promise to save your time, protect your data, and make digital healthcare truly human.

The problem today

  • Handwritten prescriptions get lost and are hard to read.
  • Doctors spend precious time on paperwork and searching records.
  • Clinics need printed or digitally shareable prescriptions to stay compliant — handwritten is no longer enough.

Our solution
